There are some experiences you never forget. The Grillon Resort offers you such experiences. Just imagine that you’re sitting on the terrace of your private villa at sunset and a manta ray suddenly swims by. Or you’re snorkelling in the sea in the morning and are greeted by a colourful and curious turtle. In Maldives you’re living on the verge of a very distinctive world: the sea is the world’s largest natural habitat. And here the colours of the corals and fish unfold their very special magic. Le Grillon is a place of dreams, both on the beach and in the water. So come and share your dreams with us! We look forward to welcoming you!
